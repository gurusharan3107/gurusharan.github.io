Chapter One

The Underdog

A young shepherd boy from Bethlehem, the youngest of eight sons, tending his father's sheep in the wilderness. Armed with nothing but a sling and his unwavering faith.

While others saw only a boy, God saw a warrior. While the army saw weakness, the Lord saw strength of heart that would change history forever.

Chapter Two

The Giant

Goliath of Gath, standing over nine feet tall, clad in bronze armor from head to toe. A Philistine champion who had taunted Israel's army for forty days and forty nights.

"Choose a man and have him come down to me. If he is able to fight and kill me, we will become your subjects. But if I kill him, you will become ours." The entire kingdom trembled at his voice.
